Master Services Agreement

Between Virginia Information Technologies Agency (VITA) and Amazon Web Services, Inc.

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of October 1, 2023 ("Effective Date") by and between the Virginia Information Technologies Agency ("VITA") and Amazon Web Services, Inc. ("Provider").

1. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver cloud storage, computing, and associated infrastructure services as detailed in Exhibit A (Statement of Work). Services must comply with all state and federal regulations concerning data privacy and security.

2. Term and Termination

The initial term of this Agreement shall be for a period of three (3) years commencing on the Effective Date.

2.1. Auto-Renewal and Notice

Unless either party provides written notice of its intent not to renew at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods. Upon any such automatic renewal, Provider's standard rates shall apply, subject to a maximum increase of 12% over the previous term's rates.

3. Data Sovereignty

All VITA data, including backups and disaster recovery instances, must physically reside within the continental United States. Provider assumes full liability for any data exfiltration resulting from unauthorized cross-border transfers.
